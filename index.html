Non-Vegetarian Food

Non-vegetarian foods are rich in protein of high biological value and in Vit B complex, especially B12 which is not available in plant foods. Fish, especially the small varieties are a rich source of calcium.Egg-white is good source of protein and easily digested. The incidence of high BP, Heart disease, Obesity and high cholesterol levels is found to be greater among non-vegetarians. Elders who suffer from these problems should reduce the intake of fleshy foods in their diet.
